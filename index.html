    FAQ

    Are the car prices cheaper than buying from the manufacturer?

    Yes, Volska is a dealership which provides you with affordable & luxury cars.

    Are the cars used?

    On the online catalog, there is a label saying if the car is used or new. You can also ask the employees in real life.

    How long can I test a vehicle?

    You can test it for an hour.

    How do I get my cars delivered?

    A driver will drive to your address, afterwards he will uber back to the dealership for the online program.

    Are the repairs free?

    The repairs aren't free but very affordable, they cost around $200 USD depending on the car & model.
